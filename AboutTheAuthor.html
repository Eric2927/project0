About the Author

Eric Li was born in Canada in 2002. He spent time in both Canada and China during his childhood, and moved to America for good in 2010. He started playing the piano when he was 4 years old, and took up french horn in 5th grade. He has made central districts on horn every year and made all-state last year. Eric now participates in one of the top groups in the Boston Youth Symphony Orchestras (BYSO) as well as the WPI orchestra.
